Instructions to use Media Library

1
When you open the Media Library, you can:
  • Add videos by going to YouTube, copying and pasting a video link into the textbox then clicking the “Save Bookmark” button.
  • Play the video by clicking on the YouTube icon.
  • Pause and restart videos by clicking on the pause and run symbols.
  • Transition to the YouTube website by clicking the "YouTube" icon.
  • Delete the video by pressing the “Remove Video” link under each video thumbnail.
2
For your convenience, you can also:
  • Adjust the Media Library window to the required size.
  • Zoom in or out the Media Library window content.
  • Make the videos appear on the full screen by clicking the full screen icon.
  • Visit “Safe Watch” recommendations if you have concerns.
